## 📝 Description

When logging into the SauceDemo website using the **problem_user** account, the product images are displayed incorrectly. Instead of showing the corresponding product images, dog images appear on the Products page.

---

## 🔄 Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the SauceDemo website.
2. Log in with:
   - **Username:** `problem_user`
   - **Password:** `secret_sauce`
3. Navigate to the **Products** page.
4. Observe the product images.

---

## ✅ Expected Result

Each product should display its corresponding product image correctly.

---

## ❌ Actual Result

Dog images are displayed instead of the correct product images.
